Who I Am

I am a dedicated California-licensed Insurance Producer focused on one mission: helping families build lasting financial security through education, protection, and smart planning.

My approach is rooted in the belief that when families understand their options and have the right protections in place, they thrive — and entire communities grow stronger. I work directly with individuals and families to create personalized strategies tailored to their goals, values, and stage of life.

I am also building a team of like-minded professionals who share this vision. Together, we are committed to raising the level of financial literacy in our communities while creating meaningful careers for those who join us.

How I Can Help You & Your Family

My focus is education first. I equip families with the knowledge and tools they need to make confident financial decisions.

Financial Education & Literacy

One-on-one guidance and community workshops that demystify budgeting, saving, credit, insurance, and long-term planning. Knowledge is the foundation of financial freedom.

Life Insurance & Family Protection

Customized life insurance strategies designed to protect your loved ones' lifestyle, cover debts, and create a lasting legacy. Peace of mind is priceless.

Retirement & Wealth Strategies

Clear, actionable plans to help you prepare for retirement, build generational wealth, and make the most of your hard-earned money through disciplined strategies.

MY WHY

Strengthening Our Community

Financial stress is one of the biggest challenges families face. My goal is to change that by making high-quality financial education and professional guidance accessible to everyone in our community — not just the wealthy.

Education First

I believe informed families make better decisions. I host workshops and provide ongoing education so people understand their options before making commitments.

Family Protection

Every family deserves to feel secure. I help parents protect their children's future and create legacies that last beyond a single generation.

Building Together
